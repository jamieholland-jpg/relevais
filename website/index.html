Questions

What referring physicians ask.

Who is Relevais for?
Adults recovering from a cardiac event or procedure who would benefit from structured, coach-supported recovery at home. Patients join by physician referral only.
What’s my involvement as a referring physician?
Minimal. You refer the patient; the Relevais care team handles onboarding, coaching, devices, and day-to-day support. You receive periodic progress summaries and are flagged if a patient needs clinical attention.
How is patient data handled?
Patient data is handled with privacy and security as a first principle and is never shared with outside sources. (Final compliance details to be confirmed with the Relevais team.)
What does it cost the patient?
Program and coverage details are shared during onboarding. Reach out through “Request a demo” and the team will walk you through how it works for your practice and your patients.
